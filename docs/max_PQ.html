Class: max_PQ

max_PQ()

new max_PQ()

Implements a max priority queue with a binary heap. Allows us to maintain a sorted deck of N items in log N time. Adapted from https://algs4.cs.princeton.edu/code/edu/princeton/cs/algs4/MaxPQ.java.html
